Submission Guidelines

We welcome short and long papers on all topics relevant to the LocalRec Call for Papers.

All submissions and reviews will be handled electronically through the EasyChair submission site: http://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=localrec2020.

Submitted papers must be uploaded in PDF format and formatted according to the standard double-column ACM SIG proceedings guidelines that also apply to the ACM SIGSPATIAL conference submissions. Additional information about formatting and style files are available online.

Long paper submissions can be up to 10 pages and short papers up to 4 pages.
